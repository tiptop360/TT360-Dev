Refund & Return Policy

At TipTop360, we are committed to providing quality products to our customers across the UAE. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for returns and refunds in accordance with UAE Federal Law No. 24 of 2006 on Consumer Protection.

Return Eligibility

We accept returns only for defective, damaged, or incorrect items within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.

Please note: We do not accept returns based on change of mind, buyer's remorse, or personal preference.

Non-Returnable Items

For health, safety, and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Opened or used toothbrushes
  • Opened or used toothpaste and dental care products
  • Opened art supplies (paints, markers, crayons, etc.)
  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items marked as final sale or clearance

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, please contact us immediately within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund after inspection.

To report a defective item, please provide:

  • Order number
  • Clear photos of the defective or damaged item
  • Description of the issue

Cash on Delivery (COD) Orders

For orders paid via Cash on Delivery, refunds for approved returns will be processed via bank transfer. You will need to provide your bank account details (IBAN, account holder name, and bank name) to receive your refund.

How to Initiate a Return

To request a return, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order:

  • WhatsApp: +971 58 515 6033
  • Email: info@tiptop360.com

Our team will review your request and provide instructions for returning the item if approved.

Return Shipping

For defective or incorrect items, TipTop360 will cover the return shipping costs. For all other eligible returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping fees.

Items must be returned to our address in RAK Free Zone, Ras Al Khaimah, UAE. Specific return address details will be provided upon approval of your return request.

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Original payment method: 7-14 business days
  • Bank transfer (for COD orders): 7-14 business days after receiving your bank details

Please note that it may take additional time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund.

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item, please return the original item (if eligible) and place a new order on our website.

Damaged During Shipping

Please inspect your order upon delivery. If the package appears damaged, please refuse delivery or note the damage with the courier. Contact us immediately with photos of the damaged package and items.
