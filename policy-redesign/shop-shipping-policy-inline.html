At TipTop360 we aim to deliver your order quickly, safely, and transparently across the United Arab Emirates. This policy explains our delivery coverage, processing times, fees, and what to expect after placing an order.

1. Delivery coverage

We currently ship to all Emirates in the UAE:

  • Dubai
  • Abu Dhabi
  • Sharjah
  • Ras Al Khaimah
  • Ajman
  • Umm Al Quwain
  • Fujairah

If your address is within the UAE, we can deliver to your doorstep.

2. Order processing time

Orders are usually processed within 1–2 business days. Processing includes order confirmation, packing, and handover to our courier partner. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.

3. Delivery timeframes

  • All Emirates: 1–2 business days after dispatch

Delivery times may vary during peak seasons, promotions, or unforeseen courier delays.

4. Cash on Delivery & payment options

We offer:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) on eligible orders
  • Credit and debit cards
  • Digital wallets where available

Available payment methods appear at checkout.

5. Shipping fees

Shipping charges (if applicable) are displayed clearly at checkout before payment is completed. Free-shipping promotions may apply for selected products or order values.

6. Order tracking

Once your order is dispatched you may receive an SMS or email confirmation, along with tracking details where available. You can also contact our support team for delivery updates.

7. Delivery attempts

Our courier partners usually attempt delivery up to two times. If delivery is unsuccessful due to an incorrect address or unavailability:

  • Our team may contact you to reschedule
  • Orders that are not accepted or are repeatedly missed may be subject to return shipping fees

8. Address accuracy

Please make sure your delivery address and phone number are accurate at checkout. TipTop360 is not responsible for delays or failed deliveries caused by incorrect or incomplete information.

9. Damaged or missing items during delivery

If your order arrives damaged or incomplete:

  1. Take clear photos of the packaging and item
  2. Contact us within 3 days of delivery
  3. Include your order number in the message

We will investigate and assist promptly. See our Refund & Return Policy for details.
