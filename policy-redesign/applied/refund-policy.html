At TipTop360 we want you to be happy with every order. We know returns are sometimes necessary, so this policy sets out clearly when and how you can return an item and receive a refund, in line with UAE Consumer Protection Law — Federal Law No. 15 of 2020.

1. Return eligibility

  • Returns are accepted within 3 days of receiving your order.
  • Items must be unused, sealed, undamaged, and in their original packaging with all accessories intact.
  • All return requests must be submitted in writing (email or WhatsApp) with your order number.

We do not accept returns based on change of mind once an item has been opened or used.

2. Non-returnable items

For health, safety, and hygiene reasons, the following cannot be returned or exchanged once opened or used:

  • Toothbrushes and electric toothbrush heads
  • Toothpaste and other dental-care products
  • Opened art supplies (paints, markers, foam, refills)
  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Customised or personalised items
  • Items marked as final sale or clearance

3. Damaged, defective, or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, contact us within 3 days of delivery. After inspection we will arrange a replacement or a full refund. Please provide:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos of the item and its packaging
  • A short description of the issue

4. How to start a return

Contact our UAE support team within 3 days of delivery:

  • WhatsApp: +971 58 515 6033
  • Email: info@tiptop360.com

We will review your request and send return instructions if it is approved.

5. Refund process & timeline

  • Once we receive and inspect your return and confirm it meets the conditions above, we issue your refund within 5–7 business days.
  • Refunds go back to your original payment method. For Cash on Delivery orders, we refund by bank transfer — we’ll ask for your IBAN, account holder name, and bank name.
  • Your bank may take additional time to post the refund to your account.
  • Shipping fees (if any) are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

6. Return shipping

For defective, damaged, or incorrect items, TipTop360 covers return shipping. For all other eligible returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. Return address details are provided once your return is approved.

7. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item, please return the original (if eligible) and place a new order.

8. Legal compliance

This policy complies with UAE Consumer Protection Law — Federal Law No. 15 of 2020. We are committed to handling every return fairly and transparently.

Refund policy FAQ

How many days do I have to return an item?

You can request a return within 3 days of receiving your order, provided the item is unused, sealed, and in its original packaging.

How long does a refund take?

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method, or by bank transfer for Cash on Delivery orders.

Can I return a toothbrush or toothpaste?

For hygiene reasons, opened or used toothbrushes, toothpaste, and dental items cannot be returned. Sealed, unused items in original packaging may be eligible.

How are Cash on Delivery orders refunded?

We refund COD orders by bank transfer. We’ll ask for your IBAN, account holder name, and bank name to process it.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Contact us within 3 days with photos of the item and packaging. After inspection we’ll arrange a replacement or full refund and cover return shipping.
