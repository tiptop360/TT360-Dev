At TipTop360 we respect your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. By using tiptop360.com or placing an order, you agree to the practices described here. This policy is aligned with the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021) and, for international shoppers, with the EU GDPR.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Name and contact details (email, phone, delivery address)
  • Billing and shipping information
  • Account login details, if you create an account
  • Order and purchase history
  • Messages you send our customer support team

Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser type
  • IP address and approximate location (e.g. which Emirate you shop from)
  • Pages viewed and how you interact with our store
  • Cookies and similar technologies

Information from trusted partners

  • Payment processors
  • Shipping and logistics couriers
  • Marketing and analytics services

2. Why we collect your information

Orders & fulfilment

  • Process payments securely (cards and Cash on Delivery)
  • Pack, ship, and deliver your order across the UAE
  • Handle returns, exchanges, and refunds
  • Send order and delivery updates

Account & experience

  • Create and manage your account
  • Remember your preferences and history
  • Improve our products and website

Marketing (optional)

  • Send offers and updates — you can unsubscribe anytime
  • Show relevant products and recommendations

Security & legal

  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with UAE laws and respond to lawful requests

3. How we share your information

We only share data when it is needed to run our business, and never to sell it. We may share information with:

  • Shopify, our e-commerce platform
  • Payment service providers
  • Shipping and fulfilment couriers
  • Analytics and marketing providers
  • Authorities, where required by UAE law

Each provider is required to handle your data responsibly under their own privacy terms.

4. Shopify and our processors

TipTop360 runs on Shopify, which provides secure hosting, checkout, and analytics. Shopify may process your data to deliver these services. Learn more in the Shopify Consumer Privacy Policy and the Shopify Privacy Portal.

5. Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies to remember preferences, improve performance, analyse traffic, and personalise recommendations. You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work without them.

6. Children’s privacy

Although we sell products for children, our store is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under the legal age in their country. Parents or guardians can contact us to review or delete a child’s information.

7. Data security and retention

We use industry-standard security measures and trusted providers to protect your information. No online system is 100% secure, so please avoid sharing sensitive details through unsecured channels. We keep your data only as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

8. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Receive a copy of your data (portability)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We may verify your identity before responding and will reply within the timeframe required by applicable law.

9. International data transfers

Your information may be processed or stored outside the UAE by our service providers. When we transfer data internationally, we apply recognised safeguards to keep it protected.

10. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or the law. The latest version will always appear on this page with a revised “last updated” date.

No. TipTop360 never sells your personal information. We use it only to operate and improve our services.

We use industry-standard security and trusted processors such as Shopify to guard your data against unauthorised access.

Can I opt out of marketing emails?

Yes. Every marketing email has an unsubscribe link, or you can contact us directly to opt out.
